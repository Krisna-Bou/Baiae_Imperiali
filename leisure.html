Exciting and thrilling activities

Chariot Racing

Chariot Races are thrilling spectacles within the great Circus Maximus. Shaped like a bullet, racers would loop around the circus whilst whipping their horses and their opponents.

There are 12 chariots which are split into teams, denoted by the colours: Red, Blue, Green and White.

Gladiator Battles

Located in the world famous Colosseum, Gladiator battles are brutal fights of man on man to the death.

Gladiators are equipped with a variety of weapons and armor to defeat their foe. It is true thrill to watch men put their lives on the line in battle.

Special events are also held in the arena, with animals being released or rarely, the Colosseum is flooded to provide for naval battles.

Olympics

Spectate upon hundreds of athletes as they participate in sports such as Running, Jumping, Discus Throw and Wrestling.

Or perhaps you would like to join in the fun, with admissions open for anyone to compete.

Calm and soothing pastimes

Countryside Tours

The Roman Empire is a vast territory, spanning hundreds of kilometers. Explore this land with our selected tour guides.

These tours bring you right to the country side, with the abbundance of nature to embrace you.

Botanical Gardens

The Empire displays many gardens, with many avaliable to the resorts visitors

Come and see the beautiful fields of flowers our slaves have worked their whole lives on.

NOTE: Trampling of flowers will incur fines

Roman Baths

Roman baths are the truest form of relaxation in the resort, with split or mixed open bathing avaliable.

No longer will these baths be unique to the elite, as they are now open to the public to use.
